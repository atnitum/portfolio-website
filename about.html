About

My Story

Hey, I’m Mala Mutinta. After spending my time in university cultivating a broad range of design skills and gaining some industry experience, I graduated without a clear idea of where I wanted to apply these skills in a professional role. Feeling stuck, I booked a one-way ticket to Central America and spent months country hopping. From this, I came to the conclusion that I needed to be in a role that would allow me to stay creative, yet also assist and improve the lives of others around me, which lead me the world of UI design. I aspire to create designs that are aesthetically pleasing, but most importantly, offer solid solutions to user problems and create a smooth, enjoyable experience for all kinds of users. When I’m not designing, I’m doodling in Procreate, trying to improve my rusty Spanish skills, or practicing for roller derby!
