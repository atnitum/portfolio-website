Veg Ahead

What is it? A web application with a variety of simple, easy and decadent plant-based meals

Who is it for? Anyone who is looking for plant-based meals to create. It’s not solely limited to vegans or vegetarians

Where is it used? At the user’s home, in their kitchen, or when they are food shopping

Why is it necessary? People who want to try cooking vegan meals may feel overwhelmed with the options already on the market or may not know where to start. A recipe web app like this will give them the chance to narrow down their searches for easy recipes that truly appeal to them.

My Role

This project was also completed for my Career Foundry Course. I designed the entirety of the project from scratch, working through the entire UI and UX process. I conducted user research, usability tests, and prototyped the finalised screens.
